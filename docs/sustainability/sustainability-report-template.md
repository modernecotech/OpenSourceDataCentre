# Sustainability Report Template

Report sections:

- reporting boundary;
- IT energy;
- facility energy;
- PUE;
- water use and WUE;
- carbon emissions and CUE;
- renewable energy fraction;
- heat reuse;
- generator fuel;
- e-waste and circularity;
- battery lifecycle;
- exceptions and estimates;
- improvement plan.

Every number should reference a meter, invoice, procurement record, or approved estimate method.
