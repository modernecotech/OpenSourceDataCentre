# PUE/WUE/CUE Measurement Boundary

Define boundaries before reporting metrics.

Required boundary fields:

- facility meter scope;
- IT load meter scope;
- cooling water meter scope;
- generator fuel scope;
- onsite renewable scope;
- imported renewable certificate scope;
- excluded office or workshop loads;
- data quality and estimation rules.

Report metric maturity as:

- design estimate;
- commissioning measurement;
- monthly operating measurement;
- annual audited report.
