# Heat Reuse Feasibility

Heat reuse is useful only when there is a real off-taker and metered evidence.

Assess:

- recoverable heat temperature;
- seasonal demand;
- distance to off-taker;
- backup heat rejection;
- metering;
- contract or public-service use;
- hygiene/safety requirements;
- economics.

Do not count theoretical heat reuse as operating sustainability evidence.
