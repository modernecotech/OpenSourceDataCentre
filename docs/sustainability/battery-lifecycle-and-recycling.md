# Battery Lifecycle and Recycling

BESS planning must include end-of-life before purchase.

Require:

- warranty and cycle-life assumptions;
- health monitoring;
- degradation plan;
- fire and transport rules;
- replacement strategy;
- recycling or takeback evidence;
- hazardous material handling;
- supplier financial and operational credibility.

Battery procurement without recycling and safety evidence is not commercially mature.
