# Operational Readiness Review

Operational readiness proves the team can safely run the site.

Review areas:

- staffing and escalation;
- MOP/SOP/EOP pack;
- permit-to-work and lockout/tagout;
- shift handover;
- incident command;
- alarm philosophy;
- monitoring and telemetry retention;
- spares and tools;
- access control;
- customer support;
- backup and restore;
- drill results;
- open risks and accepted exceptions.

No production workload should be accepted before operational readiness is signed off.
