# Design Freeze Readiness

Design freeze is the point where major assumptions become controlled.

Required before freeze:

- site scorecard accepted;
- electrical single-line basis accepted;
- DC protection and safety review path accepted;
- cooling design basis accepted;
- fire strategy path accepted;
- commercial products and SLA scope understood;
- procurement critical suppliers identified;
- risk register reviewed;
- unresolved exceptions approved by owner engineer.

Design freeze does not mean design is perfect. It means changes now require controlled review.
