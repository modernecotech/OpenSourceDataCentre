# Handover to Operations

Handover transfers the site from project delivery to operations.

Handover package:

- as-built drawings;
- commissioning records;
- defects and retest log;
- open risks and exceptions;
- training records;
- spares inventory;
- supplier contacts;
- access lists;
- monitoring dashboards;
- maintenance schedules;
- customer service catalogue;
- authority approvals;
- audit evidence register.

Handover must be accepted by operations, not merely delivered by the project team.
