# Risk and Action Tracking

Risk and action tracking keeps project gaps visible.

Source data:

- [risk-register.csv](../../data/delivery/risk-register.csv)
- [action-tracker.csv](../../data/delivery/action-tracker.csv)

Risk rules:

- assign one owner;
- record impact and likelihood;
- define treatment;
- link evidence;
- review at every gate;
- do not hide accepted residual risk.

Action rules:

- define due phase;
- link source;
- link evidence path;
- close only when evidence exists;
- move blocked actions to the risk register.
