# Authority Permit Register

The authority permit register tracks the approval path for a deployment.

Source data:

- [authority-permits.csv](../../data/delivery/authority-permits.csv)

Minimum approval families:

- planning or zoning;
- building permit;
- fire/life safety;
- electrical inspection and energization;
- fuel storage;
- BESS safety;
- telecom/carrier entry;
- environmental or water/noise conditions;
- occupancy or production launch.

Every approval must have an owner, authority contact, submission package, open questions, status, and evidence location.
