# Defects and Retest Log

Commissioning defects must be tracked until closed or formally accepted.

Fields:

- defect ID;
- test ID;
- system;
- severity;
- description;
- owner;
- corrective action;
- retest procedure;
- retest result;
- accepted exception where applicable;
- closure date.

Critical defects must not remain open at production handover unless explicitly accepted by the owner and operations lead.
