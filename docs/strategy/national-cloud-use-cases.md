# National Cloud Use Cases

Open Source Data Centre should prioritize workloads where sovereignty, continuity, and local operations matter more than access to proprietary cloud-only features.

## Public Sector

- Identity, records, licensing, tax, customs, courts, land registry, and citizen-service portals.
- Secure collaboration, document storage, email, workflow tools, and digital archives.
- National or regional backup targets for ministries and public agencies.

## Health

- Hospital information systems, imaging archives, lab systems, e-prescription services, and telemedicine platforms.
- Disaster-recovery copies for regional hospital groups.
- Data environments for public-health analytics with strict tenant and audit boundaries.

## Education and Research

- University clouds for teaching, research, simulation, AI labs, and national research networks.
- Shared GPU and HPC queues with fair-share policies.
- Open model hosting for local-language and public-interest AI systems.

## Connectivity and Edge

- ISP, telecom, and ministry edge services for DNS, caching, access gateways, observability, and local content distribution.
- Regional edge nodes for latency-sensitive public services.

## AI and HPC

- National AI inference and fine-tuning platforms.
- Scientific compute, climate modelling, geospatial workloads, and secure data rooms.
- Queued shared GPU access with transparent cost, energy, and priority policies.
