# Commercial Service Catalogue

The software service catalogue covers cloud workflows. A commercial datacentre also needs sellable facility and interconnection products.

Source data:

- [colocation-products.csv](../../data/commercial/colocation-products.csv)
- [cross-connect-products.csv](../../data/commercial/cross-connect-products.csv)
- [remote-hands-products.csv](../../data/commercial/remote-hands-products.csv)

Product families:

- rack unit, half rack, full cabinet, private cage, and private suite;
- contracted kW and A/B power feed;
- access badge and visitor approval;
- single-mode fibre, multimode fibre, copper, IP transit, IXP, and cloud-on-ramp equivalent cross-connects;
- remote hands, smart hands, visual inspection, power cycle, cable patch, and media swap;
- shipping, receiving, staging, chain of custody, and customer demarcation.

Every product needs:

- demarcation point;
- allowed customer use;
- provider responsibilities;
- required controls;
- evidence record;
- SLA class;
- pricing basis.
