# Acceptable Use Policy

Acceptable use protects the platform and other tenants.

Prohibit:

- unlawful content or activity;
- abuse, spam, scanning, or attacks;
- cryptocurrency mining unless explicitly approved;
- bypassing identity, logging, or security controls;
- unsafe facility behavior;
- unapproved radio, network, or high-power equipment;
- data processing that violates residency or public-sector policy.

Define investigation, notification, suspension, evidence preservation, and appeal paths.
