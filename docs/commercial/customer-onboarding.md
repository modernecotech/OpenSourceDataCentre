# Customer Onboarding

Customer onboarding turns commercial products into controlled service.

Required steps:

1. Identify customer legal entity and public-sector authority where applicable.
2. Define service order: rack, power, network, cloud services, access, support.
3. Assign responsibility matrix.
4. Approve data residency, key ownership, and logging requirements.
5. Approve access list and emergency contacts.
6. Define SLA class and maintenance notification path.
7. Create billing or cost-recovery record.
8. Record demarcation and acceptance evidence.

No customer should receive access before responsibility, access, incident, and exit terms are recorded.
