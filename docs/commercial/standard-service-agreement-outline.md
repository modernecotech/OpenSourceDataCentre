# Standard Service Agreement Outline

This is a contract outline, not legal advice.

Sections:

- parties and authority;
- services ordered;
- term and renewal;
- fees or cost recovery;
- customer responsibilities;
- operator responsibilities;
- SLA and credits;
- maintenance windows;
- acceptable use;
- data residency and key ownership;
- confidentiality;
- incident notification;
- audit and compliance;
- suspension and termination;
- exit and data return;
- dispute resolution;
- local law and public-sector requirements.

Local counsel must adapt this to the deployment jurisdiction.
