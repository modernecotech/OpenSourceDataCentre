# Supplier Audit Checklist

Use this checklist before relying on a supplier for critical or important systems.

## Supplier Questions

- What exact model, firmware, and revision will ship?
- What certificates, test reports, and local approvals apply?
- What parts are field-replaceable?
- What tools are required for maintenance?
- What interfaces are open and documented?
- What data leaves the country during support?
- What is the lead time for spares and warranty replacement?
- What happens if the supplier, reseller, or remote service account disappears?

## Evidence to Collect

- Datasheets and manuals.
- Wiring or interface diagrams.
- Test reports and certificates.
- Warranty terms.
- Firmware update process.
- Export control and support-access notes.
- References from comparable deployments.
