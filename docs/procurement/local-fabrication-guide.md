# Local Fabrication Guide

Local fabrication can reduce cost, lead time, and dependency, but it must not move safety-critical work outside qualified review.

## Good Candidates

- Cable trays, brackets, frames, labels, covers, and non-pressure structural supports.
- Service-trench modules and access panels.
- Rack adapters, shelves, blanking panels, and low-risk mechanical parts.
- Sensor mounts, gateway enclosures, and documentation holders.

## Poor Candidates Without Certification

- Pressure vessels.
- Fire suppression hardware.
- Life-safety systems.
- High-energy electrical protection equipment.
- Battery modules, BMS internals, and certified converter internals.
- Lifting gear and structural systems outside local engineering sign-off.

## Required Records

- Drawing or model version.
- Material and finish.
- Fabricator.
- Inspection result.
- Load, fit, or continuity test where relevant.
- Replacement or repair procedure.
