# CFD and Airflow Validation

CFD and airflow validation are required when density, layout, or climate creates uncertainty.

Use for:

- high-density racks;
- unusual room geometry;
- contained aisles;
- rear-door heat exchangers;
- mixed air/liquid rows;
- expected failure modes;
- expansion designs.

Evidence:

- model assumptions;
- boundary conditions;
- rack loads;
- failure cases;
- measured validation after commissioning;
- corrective actions.
