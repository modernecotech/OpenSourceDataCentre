# Emergency Power-Off and Isolation

Emergency power-off must protect life safety without creating avoidable hazards.

Define:

- EPO button locations;
- systems affected;
- systems intentionally not affected;
- fire-alarm interaction;
- BESS isolation;
- generator shutdown or continued emergency role;
- DC bus discharge or isolation method;
- restart authorization;
- customer notification;
- evidence and test cadence.

EPO design must be reviewed with electrical, fire, operations, and authority stakeholders.
