# Climate-Specific Cooling Selection

Cooling must match the local climate, water limits, and service capability.

| Climate | Risks | Preferred direction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| hot-dry | high dry-bulb, dust, water stress | dry coolers, filtration, liquid assist, limited evaporative use. |
| hot-humid | poor evaporative performance, corrosion | chilled water or hybrid systems, corrosion control, strong monitoring. |
| dusty | filter loading and fouling | staged filtration, easy filter access, cleaning procedure. |
| coastal-corrosive | salt corrosion | coated coils, corrosion-resistant hardware, inspection cadence. |
| cold | freezing and economizer controls | glycol/freeze protection, economizer sequence, condensation control. |
| high-altitude | lower air density | derating, airflow review, generator derating. |

The cooling selection should be recorded in the site evidence pack before design freeze.
