# Electrical Single-Line Design Basis - 250 kW Pilot

This is a design-basis template for the 250 kW regional pilot. It is not a stamped single-line diagram.

Minimum drawing pack:

- utility incomer and metering;
- fallback generator boundary and changeover;
- rectifier/converter path into the 380-400 VDC backbone;
- sodium-ion BESS interface and isolation;
- DC distribution boards, breakers, fuses, contactors, isolation monitoring, and surge protection;
- 48 VDC rack or row conversion;
- emergency power-off and controlled shutdown paths;
- EPMS metering points;
- earthing, bonding, and lightning protection references.

Commercial review questions:

| Question | Evidence needed |
| --- | --- |
| What is the maximum available fault current at every bus? | Short-circuit study. |
| Which device clears first under each credible fault? | Breaker/fuse coordination and selectivity study. |
| How is DC arc risk controlled? | Arc-flash and arc-fault mitigation study. |
| How can maintainers isolate equipment safely? | Isolation procedure, labels, lockout/tagout, and EPO logic. |
| How is the BESS made safe for fire response? | Battery fire strategy and supplier safety data. |

Required signoff:

- electrical engineer of record;
- local authority where required;
- insurer or lender reviewer where required;
- commissioning authority.
