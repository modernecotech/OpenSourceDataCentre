# Earthing, Lightning, and Surge Protection

Earthing and surge design protects people, equipment, and service continuity.

Design scope:

- main earthing terminal;
- bonding of racks, trays, plant, BESS, generator, PV structures, and metalwork;
- lightning risk assessment;
- surge protection at utility, PV, telecom, and DC distribution boundaries;
- earth fault detection;
- equipotential bonding;
- corrosion considerations;
- test points and inspection cadence.

Evidence:

- earthing drawing;
- resistance test;
- lightning protection design;
- surge protection schedule;
- inspection and maintenance procedure.
