# Battery Safety and Fire Strategy

Battery safety must connect electrical design, fire strategy, ventilation, monitoring, and emergency response.

Require:

- supplier cell and cabinet safety data;
- fault and isolation method;
- thermal monitoring;
- smoke or gas detection where applicable;
- ventilation or exhaust strategy;
- cabinet spacing and access;
- firefighter information;
- emergency stop;
- post-event isolation and quarantine;
- recycling or disposal path.

Sodium-ion may reduce some risks compared with other chemistries, but it still requires project-specific safety review.
