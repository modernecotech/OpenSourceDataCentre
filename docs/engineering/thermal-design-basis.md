# Thermal Design Basis

The thermal design basis defines the conditions the cooling system must support.

Inputs:

- climate design day;
- rack classes;
- IT load diversity;
- air and liquid supply/return temperatures;
- allowable excursion time;
- redundancy model;
- water availability;
- dust and filtration;
- coastal corrosion risk;
- maintenance mode.

Outputs:

- cooling capacity;
- airflow and liquid-flow requirements;
- rack inlet envelope;
- thermal ride-through time;
- heat-rejection strategy;
- monitoring points;
- acceptance tests.
