# Fire Strategy

This file is a fire/life-safety design-basis template, not an authority-approved fire strategy.

A project fire strategy must be produced by qualified local professionals and accepted by the authority having jurisdiction.

Minimum scope:

- detection strategy, including aspirating smoke detection where appropriate;
- alarm integration and evacuation logic;
- compartmentation and fire stopping;
- clean agent, inert gas, pre-action sprinkler, or hybrid suppression decision;
- sodium-ion BESS fire risk, cabinet spacing, exhaust/venting, supplier test data, and emergency response;
- egress and occupant safety;
- emergency power-off and firefighter interface;
- post-fire recovery and re-energization approval.

Evidence required:

- fire engineer report;
- supplier safety data;
- authority review record;
- commissioning test records;
- emergency drill records.
