# Direct-to-Chip Readiness

Direct-to-chip cooling requires facility and operations readiness.

Readiness criteria:

- liquid loop capacity;
- CDU redundancy;
- water quality control;
- leak detection;
- trained technicians;
- spare hoses and connectors;
- rack service procedure;
- emergency isolation;
- vendor support;
- fire strategy compatibility.

Do not deploy direct-to-chip equipment as a surprise tenant option. It must be a declared rack class.
