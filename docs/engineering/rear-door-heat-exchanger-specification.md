# Rear-Door Heat Exchanger Specification

Rear-door heat exchangers are a practical bridge between air and liquid cooling.

Specify:

- rack compatibility;
- heat-rejection capacity;
- water temperature and flow;
- pressure drop;
- fan or passive behavior;
- condensate risk;
- door swing and service clearance;
- leak detection;
- isolation valves;
- maintenance access.

Commissioning must verify rack inlet temperature under representative load.
