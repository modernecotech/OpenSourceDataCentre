# Cooling Failure Thermal Runway Test

This commissioning test measures how quickly rack temperatures rise under cooling failure.

Test steps:

1. Confirm safety and rollback plan.
2. Apply representative IT load.
3. Record baseline inlet, outlet, liquid, and room temperatures.
4. Simulate defined cooling failure.
5. Record temperature rise and alarm timing.
6. Verify workload-shed or emergency response.
7. Restore cooling.
8. Confirm no equipment damage.
9. Record accepted ride-through time and corrective actions.

This test must be scoped carefully to avoid damaging equipment.
