# Leak Detection and Water Treatment

Cooling loops need controlled water quality and leak response.

Leak detection:

- under-rack and row sensors;
- plant-room sensors;
- service trench sensors;
- alarm levels;
- automatic and manual response;
- tenant-impact classification;
- test cadence.

Water treatment:

- fluid chemistry;
- corrosion inhibitors;
- filtration;
- biological control where relevant;
- conductivity limits;
- sampling cadence;
- disposal rules.

Every liquid-cooling deployment needs a leak-response EOP and water-treatment log.
