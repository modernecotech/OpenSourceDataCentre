# Clean Agent vs Inert Gas

Suppression selection is site-specific.

Evaluate:

- local availability of refill and service;
- room integrity and leakage;
- occupant safety;
- environmental restrictions;
- compatibility with electrical and battery spaces;
- false discharge risk;
- maintenance cost;
- authority approval;
- recovery time after discharge.

In developing-world deployments, serviceability and refill logistics matter as much as headline suppression performance.
