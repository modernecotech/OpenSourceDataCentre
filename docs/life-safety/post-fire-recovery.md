# Post-Fire Recovery

Post-fire recovery must be controlled, not improvised.

Recovery steps:

1. Confirm site is safe and authority release is received.
2. Preserve evidence for incident investigation.
3. Isolate affected electrical and battery systems.
4. Inspect suppression, smoke, water, and corrosion impact.
5. Assess structural and fire-stopping damage.
6. Verify tenant data and backup state.
7. Approve re-energization through engineer and incident commander.
8. Run post-incident review.

No affected system should return to service without documented inspection and approval.
