# Battery Fire Risk

Sodium-ion BESS selection must include safety evidence, not only price and energy density.

Require:

- supplier fire-test data;
- cabinet spacing requirements;
- ventilation or exhaust strategy;
- thermal runaway detection;
- gas/smoke detection where applicable;
- emergency stop and isolation;
- firefighter information;
- water or suppression compatibility guidance;
- post-event quarantine and inspection procedure;
- recycling and disposal path.

No BESS should be accepted without project-specific review by electrical and fire professionals.
