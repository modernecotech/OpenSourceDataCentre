# Pre-Action Sprinkler Criteria

Pre-action sprinklers may be appropriate where local code, insurer, or fire authority requires water-based suppression.

Define:

- detection trigger logic;
- double-interlock or single-interlock selection;
- drainage and leak detection;
- water damage mitigation;
- isolation valves;
- inspection cadence;
- firefighter interface;
- post-activation recovery.

Coordinate with electrical isolation and emergency power-off logic.
