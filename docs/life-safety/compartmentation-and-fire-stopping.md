# Compartmentation and Fire Stopping

Compartmentation limits fire and smoke spread.

Evidence required:

- fire-rated wall and door schedule;
- cable and pipe penetration register;
- fire-stopping product data;
- inspection photos;
- defect closeout;
- change-control process for new penetrations;
- room integrity test where gaseous suppression is used.

Cable changes are a fire-stopping risk and should be part of the MOP/permit process.
