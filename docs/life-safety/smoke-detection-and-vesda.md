# Smoke Detection and VESDA

Early detection protects uptime and life safety.

Design areas:

- return-air and ceiling detection;
- underfloor or service trench detection where applicable;
- battery room or yard detection;
- generator/fuel area detection where applicable;
- aspirating detection sample points;
- alarm thresholds;
- maintenance and calibration;
- integration with BMS, fire alarm, EPO, and security response.

Detection must be tested during commissioning and periodically after handover.
