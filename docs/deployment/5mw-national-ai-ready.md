# 5 MW National AI-Ready

The 5 MW national/AI-ready pattern is strategic compute infrastructure. It should only follow successful pilot and regional production operations.

## Primary Goals

- Support national AI, HPC, research, and larger public-cloud workloads.
- Provide a credible local alternative for sensitive or regulated workloads.
- Operate at a scale where power availability, procurement, skills, and governance are national planning issues.

## Baseline Shape

- Approximately 160 racks, with zones for ordinary cloud, storage, GPU, HPC, and network services.
- Modular power and cooling blocks that can be maintained without full-site outage.
- AI-ready rack rows with liquid-cooling interfaces where supply chain and skills support them.
- Stronger fibre diversity, physical security, fire zones, and disaster-recovery integration.
- National identity, key management, audit, logging, and tenant-isolation controls.

## Required Maturity

- Proven operation of smaller OSDC stages.
- Formal engineering authority review and commissioning plan.
- Multi-year spares, training, support, and replacement budgets.
- Clear policy for data residency, classified or sensitive workloads, backups, logs, and administrator access.
- Capacity planning for energy tariffs, grid reinforcement, solar land, storage, fuel, water, and heat rejection.
