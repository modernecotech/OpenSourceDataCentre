# Supplier Risk Management

Sovereign datacentres depend on suppliers but should not become trapped by them.

Supplier review fields:

- supplier identity;
- product or service;
- criticality;
- country and jurisdiction;
- support terms;
- security evidence;
- safety certificates;
- firmware/software update path;
- second-source option;
- spares availability;
- exit plan;
- review date.

Critical suppliers include BESS, DC protection, generators, cooling plant, fire systems, access control, telecom carriers, and cloud platform components.
