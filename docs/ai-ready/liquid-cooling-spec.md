# Liquid Cooling Specification

Liquid cooling needs serviceability and safety evidence.

Define:

- coolant type;
- temperature and pressure range;
- CDU redundancy;
- filtration and water treatment;
- leak detection;
- dripless connector standard;
- isolation valves;
- maintenance procedure;
- emergency response;
- compatibility with fire strategy.

Every direct-to-chip or immersion deployment needs a tested MOP and leak response plan.
