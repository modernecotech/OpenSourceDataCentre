# Batch Scheduling Energy Policy

Energy-aware scheduling helps make sovereign AI infrastructure affordable.

Inputs:

- tenant priority;
- GPU availability;
- thermal headroom;
- power price;
- renewable generation;
- battery state of charge;
- deadline;
- preemptibility.

Policy outputs:

- run now;
- defer to renewable window;
- throttle or reduce concurrency;
- move to lower-density rack class;
- reject until capacity is added.
