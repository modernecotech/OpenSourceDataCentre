# Mantrap and Anti-Tailgating

High-security deployments should prevent unsupervised tailgating into critical zones.

Controls:

- mantrap, turnstile, or supervised anti-tailgating process;
- badge plus second factor where appropriate;
- alarm on forced or held doors;
- camera coverage;
- visitor escort requirement;
- emergency egress compliance.

Safety rule: anti-tailgating controls must not compromise life-safety egress.
