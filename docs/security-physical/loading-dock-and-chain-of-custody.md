# Loading Dock and Chain of Custody

The loading area is a high-risk boundary for equipment, media, and customer trust.

Controls:

- delivery pre-notification;
- identity check;
- package inspection policy;
- staging area;
- media handling;
- asset tagging;
- camera coverage;
- sign-in/sign-out;
- chain-of-custody record.

Customer media and failed disks require explicit custody and destruction/return rules.
