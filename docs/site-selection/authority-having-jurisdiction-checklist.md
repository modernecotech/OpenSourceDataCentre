# Authority Having Jurisdiction Checklist

The authority path must be known before design freeze.

Checklist:

- planning authority;
- electrical authority or utility inspector;
- fire authority;
- environmental authority;
- health and safety authority;
- telecom regulator where relevant;
- building permit route;
- fuel storage approval route;
- BESS/fire review route;
- inspection and occupancy requirements.

Record names, contacts, submission packages, expected lead times, and unresolved questions.
