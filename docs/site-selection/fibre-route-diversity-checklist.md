# Fibre Route Diversity Checklist

A sovereign datacentre should avoid becoming a single isolated building.

Checklist:

- at least two carrier options where possible;
- physically diverse routes and entries;
- meet-me room or meet-me cabinet location;
- duct and handhole access;
- route maps and rights-of-way;
- repair SLA from carriers;
- IXP or national research network opportunity;
- cross-connect product readiness;
- emergency contact and maintenance-notification process.

Red flag: one carrier, one route, one duct, no realistic second path.
