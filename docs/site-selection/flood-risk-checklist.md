# Flood Risk Checklist

Flood review must cover more than mapped river floodplain.

Checklist:

- river, coastal, flash-flood, and surface-water history;
- elevation and freeboard against credible flood levels;
- site drainage and downstream blockage risk;
- roof and yard drainage;
- cable trench and service-duct water paths;
- fuel, BESS, generator, and switchgear location;
- emergency access during heavy rain;
- local authority flood records;
- climate-change allowance where required.

Red flag: known floodplain, poor drainage, or water flow through service trenches without engineered mitigation.
