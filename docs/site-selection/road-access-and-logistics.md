# Road Access and Logistics

Road and logistics review must cover construction, operations, emergency response, fuel, and spares.

Checklist:

- heavy equipment delivery route;
- crane and unloading area;
- all-weather fuel delivery access;
- emergency vehicle access;
- secure loading dock or staging area;
- turning radius for generator, BESS, and cooling plant deliveries;
- customs/import staging assumptions;
- local spares route;
- road closure and seasonal access risks.

Red flag: no reliable access for fuel, emergency services, or replacement plant.
