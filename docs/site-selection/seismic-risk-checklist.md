# Seismic Risk Checklist

Seismic risk must be understood before structural, rack, battery, and pipework design.

Checklist:

- local seismic hazard category;
- structural engineer review;
- rack anchoring and bracing method;
- battery cabinet anchoring;
- pipework flexibility and restraints;
- cable tray and busway support;
- generator, transformer, and switchgear anchoring;
- post-event inspection procedure.

Red flag: high or moderate seismic region with no structural review.
