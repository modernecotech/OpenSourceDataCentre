# Geotechnical Requirements

Geotechnical evidence protects the project from settlement, groundwater, foundation, and buried-service surprises.

Minimum evidence:

- desktop geology and soil review;
- groundwater and flood interaction review;
- boreholes or trial pits where required;
- bearing capacity recommendation;
- settlement estimate;
- corrosive soil or aggressive groundwater screen;
- foundation recommendation for heavy plant, BESS, and generators;
- trench and duct-bank guidance.

No final foundation, service trench, or heavy-plant layout should be accepted without project-specific geotechnical review.
