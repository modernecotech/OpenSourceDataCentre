# Carrier Onboarding

Carrier onboarding defines how telecom providers enter the facility without compromising sovereignty, security, or customer demarcation.

Checklist:

- legal entity and service scope;
- route and duct entry review;
- power and space allocation;
- meet-me room demarcation;
- access and escort rules;
- emergency contact;
- maintenance-notification process;
- BGP/peering policy where relevant;
- evidence retention and patch-record integration.

Carrier neutrality requires transparent rules: the operator should not force tenants into a single preferred carrier unless the site is explicitly non-neutral.
