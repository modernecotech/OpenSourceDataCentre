# Cross-Connect Workflow

Cross-connects need disciplined workflow because they affect customer service boundaries and carrier neutrality.

Minimum workflow:

1. Customer submits service order.
2. Operator verifies authorization and demarcation.
3. Carrier or peer issues LOA/CFA where required.
4. Network owner validates route, media, speed, and optics.
5. Technician installs cable under work order.
6. Light level or cable certification is recorded.
7. Labels and patch records are updated.
8. Customer acceptance is recorded.
9. Billing and SLA clock start only after acceptance.

Evidence:

- service order;
- LOA/CFA or equivalent authorization;
- patch record;
- test result;
- photos where policy allows;
- acceptance record.
