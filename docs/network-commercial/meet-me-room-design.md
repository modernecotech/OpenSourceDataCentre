# Meet-Me Room Design

A sovereign datacentre should be carrier-neutral where possible. The meet-me room is the controlled interconnection space for carriers, IXPs, cloud-on-ramp equivalents, and customer cross-connects.

Minimum design features:

- physically controlled room or cabinet row;
- labelled meet-me panels;
- carrier demarcation racks;
- diverse cable entry paths where site conditions allow;
- fibre management, slack storage, and test access;
- route diversity records;
- access control and CCTV coverage;
- patch record and chain-of-custody workflow;
- capacity plan for ports, ducts, and cable trays.
