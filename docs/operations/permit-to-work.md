# Permit to Work

Permit-to-work controls hazardous or service-affecting work.

Permit types:

- electrical isolation;
- hot work;
- roof or height work;
- confined space;
- cooling loop work;
- fire-system impairment;
- BESS work;
- telecom room intrusive work.

Required evidence:

- requester and approver;
- scope and location;
- hazard assessment;
- isolation points;
- lockout/tagout references;
- fire watch where required;
- start and expiry time;
- closeout and restoration check.
