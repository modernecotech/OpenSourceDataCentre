# Operator Training

The operating model must assume a local team will own the site after handover.

## Skill Areas

- Electrical safety, lockout/tagout, DC bus awareness, and generator procedures.
- Cooling loops, filters, pumps, sensors, valves, heat exchangers, and leak response.
- Fire, access, CCTV, NVR, environmental monitoring, and incident escalation.
- Linux, Kubernetes, OpenStack, Ceph, Keycloak, OPA, NetBox, Prometheus/VictoriaMetrics, Grafana, and backup tooling.
- Rust portal/API operation and calculator assumptions.
- Change management, commissioning records, and emergency runbooks.

## Training Evidence

- Role matrix.
- Training syllabus.
- Practical exercises.
- Sign-off records.
- Incident drill outcomes.
- Skills gaps and retraining dates.
