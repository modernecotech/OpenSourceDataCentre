# Lockout Tagout

Lockout/tagout protects workers during hazardous energy work.

Procedure fields:

- equipment;
- energy sources;
- isolation points;
- lock owner;
- tag owner;
- verification method;
- work permit;
- restoration steps;
- closeout approval.

LOTO is mandatory for electrical, mechanical, BESS, generator, and cooling-loop work where hazardous energy is present.
