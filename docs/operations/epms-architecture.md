# EPMS Architecture

The electrical power monitoring system records electrical state and alarms.

Scope:

- utility import/export;
- generator status;
- BESS and inverter state;
- DC bus voltage and current;
- rectifiers and converters;
- rack or row power;
- breakers and protective device status;
- power quality;
- energy meters.

Evidence:

- point list;
- alarm list;
- meter calibration;
- retention policy;
- outage event logs;
- maintenance bypass records.
