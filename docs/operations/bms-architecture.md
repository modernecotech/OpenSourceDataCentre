# BMS Architecture

The building management system controls and monitors mechanical and environmental systems.

Scope:

- cooling plant;
- pumps and valves;
- leak detection;
- temperature and humidity sensors;
- air handling where used;
- alarms and trends;
- manual override states;
- integration to fire alarm where approved.

OSDC portal role:

- read status through a controlled gateway;
- correlate alarms with tenant impact;
- retain evidence;
- show capacity and risk;
- never bypass certified safety logic.
