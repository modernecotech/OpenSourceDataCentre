# Rack Power Capacity Planning

Rack power planning prevents overloads and cooling surprises.

Inputs:

- contracted power;
- breaker and PDU rating;
- measured peak and average power;
- rack class;
- redundancy model;
- cooling mode;
- airflow or liquid-cooling readiness;
- tenant workload type.

Rules:

- never sell more power than facility and cooling evidence supports;
- reserve headroom for transients and maintenance;
- model high-density AI racks separately;
- require approval for burst power.
