# Alarm Philosophy

Alarm discipline prevents alert fatigue and missed incidents.

Define:

- severity levels;
- alarm owner;
- response time;
- escalation path;
- suppression/inhibit rules;
- nuisance alarm review;
- customer-impact classification;
- evidence retention.

Alarm classes:

- safety critical;
- service impacting;
- capacity risk;
- maintenance advisory;
- informational.

Every inhibited alarm must have an owner, reason, expiry, and review.
