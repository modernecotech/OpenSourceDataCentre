# Predictive Maintenance Model

Predictive maintenance should be evidence-led and modest at first.

Inputs:

- alarm history;
- vibration or motor current where available;
- pump runtime;
- generator starts;
- battery health;
- temperature trends;
- filter pressure;
- breaker operations;
- ticket history.

Outputs:

- inspection recommendation;
- spare part recommendation;
- maintenance window suggestion;
- risk score.

Do not let a predictive model override safety procedures or manufacturer maintenance intervals.
