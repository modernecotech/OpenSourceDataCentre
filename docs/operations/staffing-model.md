# Staffing Model

Staffing depends on site size, risk, and workload criticality. Even small sites need named owners for facilities, cloud, security, backup, and procurement.

## Roles

- Site manager.
- Electrical lead.
- Mechanical and cooling lead.
- Cloud platform lead.
- Network lead.
- Security and identity lead.
- Backup and disaster-recovery owner.
- Procurement and spares owner.
- Helpdesk or tenant support owner.

## Scaling

- 50 kW: named part-time roles can work if incident coverage and vendor escalation are clear.
- 250 kW: dedicated operator coverage and on-call escalation are expected.
- 1 MW: formal shifts, change control, and production support are expected.
- 5 MW: national infrastructure staffing, security operations, and engineering governance are required.
