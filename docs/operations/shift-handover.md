# Shift Handover

Shift handover prevents operational context from being lost between teams.

Minimum handover content:

- active alarms;
- open incidents;
- planned maintenance;
- inhibited alarms or bypasses;
- security/access exceptions;
- capacity risks;
- customer-impacting tickets;
- spares or tool issues;
- weather/grid/fuel concerns;
- unresolved safety issues.

Every handover should be timestamped, signed by outgoing and incoming leads, and retained in the operations evidence system.
